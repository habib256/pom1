## вљЎ gen2c вЂ” perf (profiled 2026-06-16)

> PC-histogram profiling of GEN2 HGR Snake (`dev/projects/gen2c/game_snake_telemetry/`) during gameplay, throttle minimised to expose the *work* behind the timing. Verdict: aside from `throttle` (deliberate, 32%), the cost is the **call glue + cc65 arithmetic helpers (~31%)** spread across the per-frame path (`decsp/incsp/popax/popptr1`, `aslax3` for Г—8, `tosaddax`, `bool*`) вЂ” not a hot function. `gen2_pixrect_asm` (13%, cell drawing) is **already asm and near-optimal**. `tick` 7%, `gen2_hgr_putu` 6%. The two real levers (both **still open** вЂ” verified 2026-06-16: no `gen2_hgr_cell`, the Snake body is still rebuilt in O(length)):

- [ ] **Dedicated asm cell blitter `gen2_hgr_cell(cx, cy, set)`** `[S В· solid]` вЂ” a single asm call taking coords **in cells** (not pixels): it does the `cx*8`/`cy*8` (Г—8 = shift) **and** the 6Г—6 fill internally, removing the C wrappers `draw_cell`/`erase_cell` **+** the C wrapper for `gen2_hgr_fill_pixrect` **+** the `jsr aslax3` per cell (2 cells/frame). This is the biggest gain on the 31% of cc65 glue. To add in `dev/lib/gen2c` (the rect family is now split into `gen2_rect.c` + the hot `gen2_blit.s`, with `gen2.h` the umbrella header) alongside `gen2_pixrect_asm`; Snake wires `draw_cell`/`erase_cell` onto it.
- [ ] **Snake body as ring-buffer** `[S В· solid]` вЂ” **algorithmic (no asm)**: the `for(i=slen-1;i>0;--i){sx[i]=sx[i-1];sy[i]=sy[i-1];}` loop in `tick()` is O(length) **every tick** and grows with the snake (bulk of the cc65 array/helpers churn). Replace it with circular head/tail indices в†’ O(1) per tick. Touches `tick()` + `draw_cell` loop + collision in `GEN2Snake.c` only.

> NB: `gen2_pixrect_asm` (13%) already optimal вЂ” don't touch it. The `throttle` (32%) is the intended timing (speed control), not a bottleneck.

## рџ§© Factor the GEN2 HGR в†” TMS9918 graphics libs (3 axes)

> **Context** (analysis 2026-06-16): the C/asm libs of the two graphics cards are conceptually parallel but **duplicated and scattered**. Three C layers: neutral base `dev/lib/apple1c/apple1io.h` (shared) В· GEN2 runtime `dev/lib/gen2c/gen2.h` (rich, in-repo) В· TMS9918 runtime `dev/lib/tms9918c/*` (Nino Porcino, vendored under `dev/lib/`). **Already factored**: the *sprites* (`tools/build_hgr_sprites.py`, master TMS в†’ HGR mirror) and the *BB font on the HGR side* (`emit_bbfont.py`: fontbb.s в†’ bbfont_cp437 в†’ gen2_bbfont). The **vector geometry** (line/circle/rect/ellipse) and the **integerв†’ASCII conversion** are identical on both sides but written 2-3 times. Plan in 3 axes; Parmigiani's "one board at a time" rule в†’ **backend resolved at link time**, no function pointer.
