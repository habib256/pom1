## âšˇ gen2c â€” perf (profiled 2026-06-16)

> PC-histogram profiling of GEN2 HGR Snake (`dev/projects/gen2_snake_telemetry/`) during gameplay, throttle minimised to expose the *work* behind the timing. Verdict: aside from `throttle` (deliberate, 32%), the cost is the **call glue + cc65 arithmetic helpers (~31%)** spread across the per-frame path (`decsp/incsp/popax/popptr1`, `aslax3` for Ă—8, `tosaddax`, `bool*`) â€” not a hot function. `gen2_pixrect_asm` (13%, cell drawing) is **already asm and near-optimal**. `tick` 7%, `gen2_hgr_putu` 6%. The two real levers (both **still open** â€” verified 2026-06-16: no `gen2_hgr_cell`, the Snake body is still rebuilt in O(length)):

- [ ] **Dedicated asm cell blitter `gen2_hgr_cell(cx, cy, set)`** `[S Â· solid]` â€” a single asm call taking coords **in cells** (not pixels): it does the `cx*8`/`cy*8` (Ă—8 = shift) **and** the 6Ă—6 fill internally, removing the C wrappers `draw_cell`/`erase_cell` **+** the C wrapper for `gen2_hgr_fill_pixrect` **+** the `jsr aslax3` per cell (2 cells/frame).
