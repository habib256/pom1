Generic patcher `tools/silicon_strict_patch.py` ran across every TMS9918 project + `lib/tms9918/*.asm` (351 NOPs total: 269 Galaga, 18 Sokoban, 17 Snake, 10 Orbital Pool, 9 Connect4, 7 Logo, 6 Life, 4 Maze3D, 1 Chess, 4 lib/m1, 6 lib/m2). - [x] ~~**TMS9918 Mode 1 demoscene: Cruzer / jblang plasma port**~~ `[M · solid]` — Done 2026-05-08. `dev/projects/tms9918_plasma/` is a 6502 port of [jblang/TMS9918A/examples/plasma.asm](https://github.com/jblang/TMS9918A/blob/master/examples/plasma.asm) (itself a Z80 port of Cruzer/Camelot's *Plascii Petsma*, C64). All 12 effects + 16 palettes + auto-cycling (256 frames per effect ≈ 8.5 s on POM1, ~100 s for the full loop). Linear-phase animation only — per-row sine warping (the Z80 speed-code path) skipped to fit the 1 MHz cycle budget. Stock 4 KB Apple-1 layout (`$0280-$0BFF` code, `$0C00-$0EFF` `plasma_starts`, `$0F00-$0FFF` page-aligned `sine_table`). Image = 1 433 B. The hot loops drop `JSR tms9918_pad12` because `init_vdp_g1 → disable_sprites` puts the chip in "Mode I + no sprites" (silicon-strict floor 6 c) and the natural STA→STA gap of 19 c is already 3× above floor. README cross-links the source-of-truth chain.
- [x] ~~**5th-sprite-overflow primitive (mid-frame raster trap without /INT)**~~ `[S · solid]` — Done 2026-05-08. `dev/lib/tms9918/tms9918_5strigger.asm` exports `arm_5s_trigger` (place 5 invisible sprites at scan line A) and `wait_5s_trigger` (`BIT $CC01 / BVC` busy-loop on bit 6). Inline equivalent macro `WAIT_5S` in `tms9918.inc`. P-LAB stock leaves /INT floating, so this is the canonical way to schedule a palette split / name-table swap mid-frame without IRQ. Mirrors Daniel Vik's "Waves" MSX1 trick (which used the same flag for IRQ-free synchronisation). README section in `dev/lib/tms9918/README.md`. Consumer demo: `dev/projects/tms9918_split/` (574 B image) renders 32 vertical stripes with a "cool" palette on the upper half, "warm" palette on the lower half — the swap fires on `WAIT_5S` after `arm_5s_trigger(96)`.
- [x] ~~**Sprite cloning (Bug N°8) visual regression fixture**~~ `[S · solid]` — Done 2026-05-08. `dev/projects/tms9918_clone/` (615 B image) is the standalone visual variant of `tms9918_siltest` T16. Boots in legal Mode I with a 3-strate striped background and 8 animated "torch" sprites at Y=80; **SPACE** toggles into illegal hybrid M1+M2 (set R0 bit 1 + R1 bit 4) and clones cascade in the top strate at Y={7,15,23,31,39,47,55,63} per POM1's formula `Y_polluted = ((slot*8) ^ ((R3 & $60) | (R6 & $07))) & $3F`. **SPACE** again returns to Mode I (clones disappear). The toggle makes side-by-side comparison trivial without rebooting. Validates commit `fa90f12` (sprite cloning detection, 2026-05). README cross-links openMSX issue #593 and silicon-strict caveats (Toshiba/V9938 don't clone, NMOS thermal drift not modelled).
