## тЪб gen2c тАФ perf (profiled 2026-06-16)

> PC-histogram profiling of GEN2 HGR Snake during gameplay. Aside from the deliberate `throttle` (32%), the cost is cc65 call glue + arithmetic helpers (~31%) spread across the per-frame path. `gen2_pixrect_asm` (13%) is already asm and near-optimal тАФ **don't touch it**. Two real levers:

- [ ] **Dedicated asm cell blitter `gen2_hgr_cell(cx, cy, set)`** `[S ┬╖ solid]` тАФ one asm call taking coords **in cells**: does the `cx*8`/`cy*8` (shift) and the 6├Ч6 fill internally, removing the C wrappers `draw_cell`/`erase_cell` + the `jsr aslax3` per cell. Biggest gain on the cc65-glue 31%. Add to `dev/lib/gen2c` alongside `gen2_pixrect_asm`; Snake wires `draw_cell`/`erase_cell` onto it.
- [ ] **Snake body as ring-buffer** `[S ┬╖ solid]` тАФ algorithmic: the `for(i=slen-1;i>0;--i)` shift in `tick()` is O(length) every tick. Replace with circular head/tail indices тЖТ O(1). Touches `tick()` + `draw_cell` loop + collision in `GEN2Snake.c` only.
