Codetank_Nyan.rom — Nyan Cat (full 12-frame Mode III animation)
  Lower jumper: 4000R → TMS_Nyan_CodeTank
                Renders the full Nyan Cat animation in TMS9918
                Mode III (Multicolor, 64×48 colored 4×4 blocks).
                12 frames cycle at ~20 fps, RLE-compressed
                (18 432 B raw → 6 830 B in ROM, 37 % size).
                ESC = exit to Wozmon.
                Algorithm and frames from J.B. Langston's z80
                port (jblang/TMS9918A); artwork is *Passan
                Kiskat* by Dromedaar Vision.
                See dev/projects/tms9918_nyan_codetank/README.md.
  Upper jumper: reserved (4000R yields a blank screen — flip
                back to Lower)
